About Me

Acquaintance

My name is Muhamad Ilhan Revaliana Firmansyah. For now, my hobby is watching anime. I entered the world of information technology and started to explore it when I graduated from high school. At the time, I chose to be a Front-End Web Developer because I saw that being a back-End Developer was too hard and difficult for me. But time after time and day after day, I wanted to be a Back-End Developer and a Full-Stack web developer when I entered the world of information technology and started studying information technology.

My first salary in information technology

When I was studying information technology, I entered UKM Tahungoding. At that time, I went through the day as usual while learning to code front-end, back-end, and other college assignments. When I started the even-to-odd semester holidays, the head of my university offered me a job at the NOIU Event Organizer company. I tried to be brave and took the front-end web developer job in the hope that I could develop and find out how to work together with the back-end team. Day after day, I was able to finish it well, even though there were many errors, and when it was done, I got my first salary.
